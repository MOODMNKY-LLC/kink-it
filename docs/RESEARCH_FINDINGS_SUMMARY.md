- Protocols reinforce power dynamic
- Create structure and reduce anxiety
- Build connection and trust
- Should be consistent, meaningful, adaptable
- Support mental health, not burden
- Must feel like obeying Dominant, not checklist

### Key Findings:

1. **Traffic Light System (Green/Yellow/Red)**
   - Green: Good, focused, ready
   - Yellow: Some concerns, need support
   - Red: Distressed, overwhelmed, need immediate attention
   - Blue: Emotional need to stop (non-physical)

2. **Daily Check-Ins**
   - Morning greeting with energy level
   - Evening reflection (optional)
   - Status updates throughout day

3. **Weekly Reviews**
   - Reflection on the week
   - What worked, what didn't
   - Adjustments needed

#### Conversation Prompts:

- Reflection prompts (growth, protocols)
- Scene debrief prompts (feedback, aftercare)
- Growth prompts (evolution, changes)

#### Scene Debriefs:

- What to adjust
