# Kink Identity Overview

**Last Updated**: 2026-01-29

---

## What is Kink Identity?

Kink identity is how you express who you are within the BDSM/kink community. It includes subtypes, dynamic intensity, relationship structures, and other aspects of your kink self.

---

## Components

### Kink Subtypes

Specific roles or identities within BDSM:
- **Submissive**: Brat, Little, Pet, Slave, Masochist, etc.
- **Dominant**: Daddy, Mommy, Master, Mistress, Sadist, etc.
- **Switch**: Switch, Versatile

### Dynamic Intensity

How deeply power exchange is integrated:
- **Casual**: Occasional scenes
- **Part-Time**: Regular but not constant
- **Lifestyle**: Integrated into daily life
- **24/7**: Full-time power exchange
- **TPE**: Total Power Exchange

### Dynamic Structures

Types of relationship structures:
- D/s, M/s, Owner/Pet, CG/l, Primal, etc.

### Experience Level

Self-reported experience:
- Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, Expert

## Benefits

1. **Self-Expression**: Express your authentic kink identity
2. **Matching**: Help others understand compatibility
3. **Communication**: Facilitate open discussions
4. **Community**: Connect with like-minded people
5. **Growth**: Track your evolution over time

---

## Privacy

You control who sees your kink identity:
- **Public**: Bond members can see it
- **Private**: Only you can see it
