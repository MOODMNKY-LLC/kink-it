# Welcome to KINK IT

**D/s Relationship Management Application**

---

## What is KINK IT?

KINK IT is a comprehensive platform designed to help Dominants and submissives manage their D/s relationships. Whether you're in a casual dynamic or a 24/7 Total Power Exchange relationship, KINK IT provides tools to enhance communication, track progress, and deepen your connection.

---

## Key Features

### Bonds System

Connect with your partner(s) through bonds - relationship groups that support dyads, polycules, households, and flexible dynamics. Create or join bonds with unique invite codes, manage members, and access bond-specific features.

### Task Management

Assign tasks, track completion, and manage rewards. Set point values, require proof, and monitor progress across your dynamic.

### Kink Identity

Express your authentic kink identity with comprehensive subtypes, dynamic intensity levels, and relationship structures. Share as much or as little as you're comfortable with.

### Notion Integration

Sync with Notion to maintain comprehensive records, contracts, and documentation. All your dynamic data stays organized and accessible.

### Discord Integration

Receive real-time notifications about task completions, rewards, and important updates directly in your Discord server.

### Analytics & Insights

Track your dynamic's progress, identify patterns, and gain insights into your relationship's health and growth.
