## Introduction

The Calendar module helps you schedule scenes, rituals, check-ins, task deadlines, and milestones. It provides structure and predictability, ensuring important events don't get missed and helping you plan your dynamic effectively.

---

## What Is the Calendar?

The Calendar includes:
- **Scene Events**: Planned BDSM scenes
- **Ritual Events**: Regular rituals and ceremonies
- **Task Deadlines**: Due dates for tasks
- **Check-In Events**: Scheduled check-ins
- **Milestone Events**: Important dates and anniversaries

## Creating Calendar Events

### For Both Partners

#### Step 1: Choose Event Type

**Scene Events**: Planned BDSM scenes
- Duration: Typically 30-90 minutes
- Includes: Activities planned, aftercare time
- Reminders: 1-2 hours before

**Ritual Events**: Regular rituals
- Examples: Weekly reviews, monthly ceremonies
- Duration: Varies by ritual
- Reminders: 1 day before typically

**Task Deadlines**: Due dates
- Linked to tasks
- Reminders: 2 hours before typically
- Helps track completion

**Check-In Events**: Scheduled check-ins
- Regular emotional check-ins
- Can be daily, weekly, monthly
- Reminders: 1 hour before

**Milestone Events**: Important dates
- Anniversaries
- Achievements
- Special occasions
- All-day events typically

#### Step 2: Set Event Details

**Title**: Clear, descriptive name
- "Evening Scene"

- "Bond Anniversary"

- Activities planned
- Purpose of event
- What to prepare

**Date & Time**: When it happens
- Start date/time

**Reminders**: When to notify
- Minutes before event

- Custom timing

#### Step 3: Add to Calendar

1. Fill in event details
2. Set reminders

## Scheduling Best Practices

### For Dominants

1. **Plan Ahead**: Schedule scenes and rituals in advance
2. **Set Reminders**: Don't rely on memory
3. **Include Buffer Time**: Allow for preparation and aftercare
4. **Review Regularly**: Check upcoming events weekly
5. **Be Flexible**: Adjust when life happens

### For Submissives

1. **Check Calendar**: Know what's coming
2. **Prepare Accordingly**: Get ready for scheduled events
3. **Respect Deadlines**: Complete tasks on time
4. **Communicate Conflicts**: If scheduling issues, say so
5. **Be Present**: Show up prepared and ready

### Calendar Management

1. **Regular Review**: Check weekly
2. **Update Promptly**: Change dates as needed
3. **Set Realistic Times**: Don't over-schedule
4. **Balance**: Mix scenes, rituals, and downtime
5. **Communicate**: Discuss schedule together

## Troubleshooting

### "Too Many Events"

**Problem**: Calendar feels overwhelming.

**Solution**:
- Reduce number of events
- Focus on what matters
- Combine similar events
- Review and prioritize

### "Events Keep Getting Missed"

**Problem**: Not seeing reminders or forgetting events.

**Solution**:
- Enable multiple reminder types
- Check calendar regularly
- Set more reminders
- Discuss schedule together

### "Can't Find Time for Everything"

**Problem**: Schedule feels too full.

**Solution**:
- Prioritize important events
- Reduce frequency if needed
- Be flexible with timing
- Communicate capacity
