**Last Updated**: 2026-02-15  
**Version**: 1.0

## Introduction

The Analytics module provides insights into your dynamic's patterns, consistency, and growth. Analytics help Dominants understand their leadership effectiveness and Submissives see their progress in a supportive, non-shaming way. Analytics are calculated from your actual data, providing real insights into your dynamic.

---

## What Is Analytics?

**Analytics** show patterns and trends in:
- Task completion rates
- Consistency over time
- Communication frequency
- Growth and development
- Dynamic health indicators

**Key Principle**: Analytics support understanding, not judgment. They help identify patterns and opportunities for growth without creating pressure or shame.

## Analytics Principles

### Non-Shaming Design

**Approach**:
- Supportive, not punitive
- Growth-focused
- Understanding-oriented
- Encouraging

**Avoid**:
- Shaming for low numbers
- Pressure to perform
- Comparison to others
- Judgment-based metrics

### Presence-Based

**Design**:
- Reflects actual engagement
- Not automated metrics
- Meaningful patterns
- Real dynamic health

### Growth-Oriented

**Focus**:
- Progress over time
- Development patterns
- Consistency trends
- Milestone recognition

## Troubleshooting

### "Analytics Feel Like Pressure"

**Problem**: Metrics feel like performance pressure.

**Solution**:
- Focus on growth, not numbers
- Use insights supportively
- Adjust if creating pressure
- Communicate concerns
- Remember: support, not judgment

### "Don't Understand Metrics"

**Problem**: Unsure what metrics mean.

**Solution**:
- Review guide documentation
- Ask Dominant to explain
- Focus on trends, not numbers
- Use insights constructively
- Discuss together

### "Numbers Don't Reflect Reality"

**Problem**: Metrics don't match actual experience.

**Solution**:
- Discuss discrepancies
- Adjust expectations
- Focus on quality over quantity
- Use insights as one data point
- Trust your experience
