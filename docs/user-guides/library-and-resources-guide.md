## Adding Resources

### For Dominants

#### Step 1: Find Resource

1. **Discover**: Find valuable content
2. **Evaluate**: Check quality and relevance
3. **Consider**: Is it appropriate for your dynamic?
4. **Verify**: Check safety and accuracy

#### Step 2: Add to Library

1. **Select Type**: Article, book, video, etc.
2. **Choose Category**: Education, safety, etc.
3. **Add Details**: Title, description, URL
4. **Rate**: 1-5 star rating
5. **Add Notes**: Why it's valuable
6. **Tag**: Relevant tags
7. **Save**: Add to library

#### Step 3: Share with Partner

1. **Assign**: Share with submissive
2. **Discuss**: Talk about why it's relevant
3. **Use Together**: Review together
4. **Apply**: Use learnings in dynamic

### For Submissives

#### Finding Resources

1. **Browse Library**: Explore available resources
2. **Search**: Find specific topics
3. **Filter**: By type or category
4. **Read/Watch**: Consume content
5. **Discuss**: Talk about learnings

#### Requesting Resources

1. **Identify Need**: What do you want to learn?
2. **Request**: Ask Dominant to find resources
3. **Review Together**: Discuss content
4. **Apply**: Use learnings

## Troubleshooting

### "Too Many Resources"

**Problem**: Library feels overwhelming.

**Solution**:
- Focus on relevant resources
- Use search and filters
- Prioritize by need
- Review gradually

### "Don't Know Where to Start"

**Problem**: Unsure which resources to use.

**Solution**:
- Start with safety guides
- Ask Dominant for recommendations
- Focus on your needs
- Build knowledge gradually

### "Resources Don't Match Our Dynamic"

**Problem**: Resources don't fit your specific dynamic.

**Solution**:
- Adapt learnings to your dynamic
- Discuss what applies
- Modify as needed
- Focus on principles, not specifics
