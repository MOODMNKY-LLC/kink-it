**Last Updated**: 2026-02-15  
**Version**: 1.0

## Introduction

The Rewards module allows Dominants to recognize and reward Submissives for their service, consistency, and growth. Rewards reinforce positive behavior, acknowledge effort, and strengthen your dynamic through meaningful recognition. This module supports various reward types and integrates with your partner's love languages.

---

## What Are Rewards?

**Rewards** are forms of recognition given by Dominants to Submissives for:
- Task completion
- Consistent service
- Growth and improvement
- Special achievements
- Exceptional effort

Rewards can be:
- **Verbal**: Words of acknowledgment and praise
- **Points**: Numerical rewards that accumulate
- **Relational**: Quality time, acts of service, physical touch
- **Achievement**: Milestone recognition
- **Permission-Based**: Special privileges earned

## Creating Rewards

### For Dominants

#### Step 1: Choose Reward Type

**Verbal Acknowledgment**:
- Words of affirmation
- Praise and recognition
- Love language: words_of_affirmation
- No points, pure recognition

**Points Reward**:
- Numerical reward
- Accumulates in points ledger
- Can be redeemed later
- Immediate or delayed

**Relational Reward**:
- Quality time together
- Acts of service
- Physical touch
- Love language specific

**Permission-Based Reward**:
- Special activity permissions
- Choice in scenes
- Privileges earned
- Autonomy within boundaries

**Achievement Reward**:
- Milestone recognition
- Consistency badges
- Growth acknowledgment
- Special celebrations

#### Step 2: Define the Reward

**Title**: Clear name
- "Excellent Service Recognition"
- "Task Completion Bonus"
- "Extended Quality Time"

**Description**: What the reward is
- Detailed explanation
- What it includes
- How it's received

**Point Value** (if points reward):
- Task completion: 10-25 points
- Bonuses: 50-100 points
- Milestones: 100+ points

**Love Language** (if relational):
- Words of affirmation
- Quality time
- Physical touch
- Acts of service
- Receiving gifts

#### Step 3: Assign Reward

1. **Select Recipient**: Choose submissive
2. **Set Status**: Available, completed, etc.
3. **Link to Task** (optional): Connect to specific task
4. **Assign**: Reward is available
5. **Acknowledge**: When received

### For Submissives

#### Receiving Rewards

When you receive a reward:
1. **Acknowledge**: Thank your Dominant
2. **Understand**: Know what you earned
3. **Appreciate**: Recognize the recognition
4. **Use**: If redeemable, use when ready
5. **Express Gratitude**: Show appreciation

#### Points System

**Earning Points**:
- Task completion
- Consistency bonuses
- Special achievements
- Manual awards

**Using Points**:
- Track in points ledger
- View point history
- Understand point values
- Redeem for rewards (if applicable)

## Best Practices

### For Dominants

1. **Recognize Regularly**: Frequent acknowledgment builds connection
2. **Match Love Languages**: Use partner's preferred language
3. **Be Specific**: Specific praise is more meaningful
4. **Vary Rewards**: Mix types for engagement
5. **Acknowledge Effort**: Recognize effort, not just completion
6. **Be Present**: Relational rewards require presence

### For Submissives

1. **Receive Graciously**: Accept recognition with gratitude
2. **Express Appreciation**: Thank your Dominant
3. **Understand Purpose**: Know why you're being recognized
4. **Use Points**: Track and understand your points
5. **Communicate Preferences**: Share what rewards mean most

### Reward Design Principles

1. **Meaningful**: Rewards should matter to recipient
2. **Proportional**: Match reward to achievement
3. **Varied**: Mix types for engagement
4. **Timely**: Give recognition promptly
5. **Specific**: Clear about what's being recognized

## Troubleshooting

### "Rewards Feel Generic"

**Problem**: Rewards don't feel personal or meaningful.

**Solution**:
- Match to love languages
- Be specific in descriptions
- Personalize to submissive's preferences
- Vary reward types

### "Don't Know What Rewards to Give"

**Problem**: Unsure what rewards would be meaningful.

**Solution**:
- Ask your submissive what they value
- Match to love languages
- Observe what they appreciate
- Start simple, build complexity

### "Points Don't Feel Meaningful"

**Problem**: Points system feels like gamification.

**Solution**:
- Combine with verbal recognition
- Explain what points represent
- Use points for meaningful rewards
- Focus on recognition, not just points
