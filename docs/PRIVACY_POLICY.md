# KINK IT Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: February 2, 2026  
**Effective Date**: February 2, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to KINK IT. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our D/s relationship management application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: Email address, display name, profile information
- **Relationship Data**: Bond information, partner connections, dynamic roles
- **Content**: Tasks, rules, boundaries, contracts, journal entries, calendar events
- **KINKSTER Characters**: Character profiles, avatars, stats, preferences
- **Notion Integration**: API keys (encrypted), workspace connections
- **Communication**: Messages, check-ins, feedback

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the app, features used, time spent
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, browser information
- **Log Data**: IP addresses, access times, error logs
- **Cookies and Tracking**: Session data, preferences, authentication tokens

### 1.3 Third-Party Information

- **Notion Data**: When you connect Notion, we access data you've explicitly shared with your integration
- **Authentication**: OAuth tokens from Notion and other providers

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### 2.1 Core Functionality

- **Service Delivery**: Provide D/s relationship management features
- **Data Synchronization**: Sync data between KINK IT and your Notion workspace
- **AI Assistance**: Enable Kinky Kincade (AI assistant) to provide personalized guidance
- **Character Management**: Store and manage your KINKSTER characters and avatars

### 2.2 Communication

- **Notifications**: Send you updates about tasks, rewards, and important events
- **Support**: Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
- **Service Updates**: Inform you about changes to our services

### 2.3 Improvement & Analytics

- **Analytics**: Understand how users interact with the app to improve features
- **Error Tracking**: Identify and fix bugs and technical issues
- **Research**: Analyze usage patterns (anonymized and aggregated)

---

## 3. Data Storage & Security

### 3.1 Encryption

- **At Rest**: All sensitive data, including API keys, is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
- **In Transit**: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses TLS/SSL encryption
- **Database**: Supabase provides enterprise-grade security and encryption

### 3.2 Access Controls

- **Row-Level Security (RLS)**: Database-level security ensures users can only access their own data
- **Authentication**: Secure authentication via Supabase Auth
- **API Keys**: Encrypted storage with separate encryption keys

### 3.3 Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Data is retained while your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Backups**: Encrypted backups are retained for disaster recovery purposes

---

## 4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

### 4.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

### 4.2 Service Providers

We may share data with trusted service providers who help us operate the app:

- **Supabase**: Database and authentication services
- **Vercel**: Hosting and deployment services
- **Notion**: When you explicitly connect your Notion workspace
- **AI Services**: For Kinky Kincade AI assistant functionality

All service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.

### 4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to:

- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect our rights and safety
- Prevent fraud or abuse
- Respond to government requests

### 4.4 Consent-Based Sharing

- **Bond Members**: Data shared within bonds is visible to bond members based on your bond's privacy settings
- **Partner Connections**: When you connect with a partner, relevant data may be shared based on your dynamic's configuration

## 5. Your Rights & Choices

### 5.1 Access & Correction

- **View Your Data**: Access all your data through the app interface

### 5.2 Deletion

## 6. Notion Integration Privacy

### 6.1 What We Access

- Only pages and databases you explicitly share with your Notion integration
- Data necessary for syncing (tasks, calendar events, etc.)
- Template structure for database discovery

### 6.2 How We Use Notion Data

- **Sync**: Bidirectional sync between KINK IT and your Notion workspace

- **Revoke Access**: Delete your Notion integration in Notion or remove API keys in KINK IT

- **No Unauthorized Access**: We can only access what you've explicitly granted
