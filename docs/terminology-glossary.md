# KINK IT Terminology Glossary

**Last Updated**: 2026-01-29

---

## BDSM/Kink Terms

### Basic Terms

**BDSM**: Bondage, Discipline, Dominance, Submission, Sadism, Masochism

**D/s**: Dominance/submission dynamic

**M/s**: Master/slave dynamic

**Kink**: Non-conventional sexual or sensual activities

**Scene**: A BDSM play session

**Safeword**: A pre-agreed word used to stop or pause play

**Aftercare**: Care provided after a scene to help participants return to baseline

---

## Bond Terms

**Bond**: A relationship group connecting members of a D/s dynamic

**Dyad**: A two-person relationship (traditional D/s pair)

**Polycule**: A multi-person polyamorous network

**Household**: A traditional leather family/household structure

**Dynamic**: A flexible power exchange structure

**Invite Code**: A unique code used to join a private bond

**Founder**: The person who created a bond

---

## Role Terms

**Dominant (Dom/Domme)**: Takes a controlling role in D/s dynamics

**Submissive (Sub)**: Submits to partner's control in D/s dynamics

**Switch**: Enjoys both dominant and submissive roles

**Master**: High protocol dominant in M/s dynamics

**Mistress**: Female-identifying master

**Slave**: Service-oriented submissive in M/s dynamics

## Subtype Terms

### Submissive Subtypes

**Brat**: Playfully resistant submissive

**Little/Middle**: Age play identity (little = child persona, middle = teen persona)

**Pet**: Pet play identity (pup, kitten, pony, etc.)

**Slave**: Service-oriented, high protocol submissive

**Masochist**: Enjoys receiving pain/sensation

**Service Sub**: Service-oriented submission

**Primal Prey**: Primal play prey role

**Rope Bunny**: Enjoys rope bondage

**Exhibitionist**: Enjoys being seen/showcased

**Degradation Sub**: Enjoys degradation/humiliation

### Dominant Subtypes

**Daddy**: Caregiver dominant (DD/lg)

**Mommy**: Caregiver dominant (MD/lb)

**Master**: High protocol, structured control

**Mistress**: Female-identifying master

**Sadist**: Enjoys giving pain/sensation

**Rigger**: Rope bondage specialist

**Primal Predator**: Primal play predator role

**Owner**: Ownership-focused dominant

**Handler**: Pet play handler/trainer

**Degradation Dom**: Enjoys degradation/humiliation

## Dynamic Terms

**24/7**: Full-time power exchange

**TPE**: Total Power Exchange

## Safety Terms

**SSC**: Safe, Sane, and Consensual

**RACK**: Risk-Aware Consensual Kink

**PRICK**: Personal Responsibility, Informed, Consensual Kink

**Hard Limit**: Activity you will never do

**Soft Limit**: Activity you might try under certain conditions

**Traffic Light System**: Green (go), Yellow (ease up), Red (stop)

---

## Relationship Terms

**Partner**: Your D/s partner

**Metamour**: Your partner's other partner (in polyamory)

**Polycule**: Network of polyamorous relationships

**Monogamous**: Exclusive relationship with one partner

**Polyamorous**: Multiple consensual relationships

**Open Relationship**: Non-exclusive relationship

---

## Play Terms

**Scene**: A BDSM play session

**Top**: Person giving/doing in a scene

**Bottom**: Person receiving in a scene

**Edge Play**: High-risk BDSM activities

**Impact Play**: Activities involving hitting/striking

**Bondage**: Restraint activities

**Discipline**: Training and correction

**Protocol**: Rules and rituals in a dynamic

---

## Consent Terms

**Consent**: Agreement to engage in activities

**Enthusiastic Consent**: Eager, positive agreement

**Informed Consent**: Agreement with full understanding

**Ongoing Consent**: Consent that can be withdrawn

**Negotiation**: Discussion of activities and limits

**Check-in**: Regular communication about dynamic

## Community Terms

**Munch**: Casual social gathering of kinksters

**Dungeon**: BDSM play space

**Event**: Organized kink gathering

**Workshop**: Educational kink event

**Community**: Local or online kink group

**Leather**: Traditional BDSM subculture

## KINK IT Specific Terms

**Bond**: Relationship group in KINK IT

**Dynamic Role**: Your role (dominant/submissive/switch)

**Submission State**: Current state of submission (active/low energy/paused)

**Task**: Assigned activity in your dynamic

**Rule**: Established guideline in your dynamic

**Reward**: Positive reinforcement in your dynamic

**Point System**: Gamification of task completion
